Detect Any Disease

Our model can take one look at a medical image and highlight any regions that contain diseases or other anomalies.

GANs

We use a Generative Adversarial Network to reconstruct a scan of a healthy organ and compare it to the organ we're looking at to spot any differences.

Encoders

The encoder is trained to turn healthy images of lungs into latent space (compact number expectation) that the GAN can use to reconstruct an organ.

Saving Doctors' Time

A breast detection model will never detect pneumonia if it’s in an image but our project can. This will save doctor’s a ton of time or catch their mistakes when they overlook something.
